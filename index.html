About Me

As you can tell I love anything that can get my creative juices flowing. I find myself the happiest when I can dive into a project and let my mind run wild. I love building and creating things, being able to find different mediums to allow my creativity to expand is my mission.

With the web growing so much and everyone turning to the web to find everything from a babysitter to a piano repair person. The web is allowing everyone to connect and small businesses being given a fight chance the web is where it is at. Learning web development will just open new career opportunities ,but it will allow me to build my brand with all my personal projects that I am involved in.
